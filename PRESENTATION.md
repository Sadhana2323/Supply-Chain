## Slide 3: Our Solution - Control Tower

### Four AI-Powered Modules:

1. **Live Risk Dashboard**
   - 24-72 hour advance warning
   - Weather, geopolitical, congestion monitoring
   - Color-coded risk levels

2. **DNA Visualizer**
   - Multi-tier supplier mapping
   - Single-point-of-failure detection
   - Geographic risk overlay

3. **Cascade Simulator**
   - Monte Carlo simulation (1000 iterations)
   - Predicts downstream impact
   - Day-by-day timeline

4. **What-If Optimizer**
   - Tests multiple strategies
   - AI recommends optimal response
   - Cost-resilience balance
